Ghanian Akan names

The significance attached to names and naming in Ghanian society is remarkable! The Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born. Frequently, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
These "day names" have further meanings concerning the character of a person.

Here are Ghanian male day names; Kwasi, Kwadwo, Kwabena, Kwaku, Yaw, Kofi and Kwame. Some examples of the Ghanian female day names include; Akosua, Adwoa, Abenaa, Akua, Yaa, Afua and Ama.
